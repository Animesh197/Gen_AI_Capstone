Wheat crops suffering from low nitrogen levels often show reduced vegetative growth and lower yield. 
Farmers should apply nitrogen-rich fertilizers such as urea.

Rice crops experiencing insufficient rainfall face water stress which significantly reduces yield. 
Irrigation methods such as drip or sprinkler systems can help maintain water supply.

Maize crops exposed to high temperatures may suffer from heat stress, reducing grain formation. 
Using heat-resistant crop varieties can improve productivity.

Low phosphorus in soil reduces root development and crop yield. 
Applying DAP fertilizer can improve phosphorus levels in soil.
